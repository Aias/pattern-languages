## Problem

"Decor" and the conception of "interior design" have spread so widely, that very often people forget their instinct for the things they really want to keep around them.

## Solution

Do not be tricked into believing that modern decor must be slick or psychedelic, or "natural" or "modern art", or "plants" or anything else that current taste-makers claim. It is most beautiful when it comes straight from your lifeâ€”the things you care for, the things that tell your story.
