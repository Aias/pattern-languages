## Problem

The instinct to climb up to some high place, from which you can look down and survey your world, seems to be a fundamental human instinct.

## Solution

Build occasional high places as landmarks throughout the city. They can be a natural part of the topography, or towers, or part of the roofs of the highest local buildingâ€”but, in any case, they should include a physical climb.
