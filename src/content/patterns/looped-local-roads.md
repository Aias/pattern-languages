## Problem

Nobody wants fast through-traffic going by their homes.

## Solution

Lay out local roads so that they form loops. A loop is defined as any stretch of road which makes it impossible for cars that don't have destinations on it to use as a shortcut. Do not allow any one loop to serve more than 50 cars, and keep the road really narrowâ€”17 to 20 feet is quite enough.
